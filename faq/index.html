General Questions

What is Ashes?

Ashes is a browser extension that replaces your default new tab page with a live countdown of your remaining time, based on your birthdate and expected lifespan. It serves as a "memento mori" — a reminder that life is finite, and every second matters.

Unlike typical new tab pages that can be distracting or cluttered, Ashes provides a minimalist, meaningful reminder that helps you live more intentionally and make conscious decisions about how you spend your time online.

Why is it called "Ashes"?

The name "Ashes" refers to the ancient phrase "ashes to ashes, dust to dust" — a reminder of mortality. It symbolizes the impermanence of human life and the value of the time we have.

Just as ashes represent what remains after a fire has burned out, the extension serves as a reminder that our time is constantly burning away, encouraging us to use it wisely while it lasts.

Is Ashes meant to be depressing?

Not at all! While confronting our mortality might seem uncomfortable at first, Ashes is designed to be empowering, not depressing. By acknowledging that our time is limited, we're more likely to:

  • Make conscious choices about how we spend our time
  • Prioritize what truly matters to us
  • Avoid wasting time on things that don't align with our values
  • Live more deliberately and with greater awareness

Many users report that after the initial adjustment period, they find the reminder liberating and clarifying rather than negative.

Who created Ashes?

Ashes was created by Aryan Techie, founder of AROICE. It was built to help people live more deliberately in the digital age, especially given how much time we spend in browser tabs.

The project was inspired by the creator's personal need for a tool that would transform mindless browsing into moments of reflection and intentionality.

You can learn more about the creator at me.aroice.in or aroice.in.

Features & Usage

How does Ashes calculate my remaining time?

Ashes uses your birthdate and an estimated lifespan to calculate your remaining time. The calculation follows these steps:

  1. You enter your date of birth when setting up the extension
  2. You select an estimated lifespan (default is based on global average life expectancy, but you can customize it)
  3. The extension calculates the difference between your projected end date and the current date/time
  4. This remaining time is displayed in various units (years, months, days, hours, etc.)

The timer updates in real-time, providing a continuous reminder of your passing moments.

Can I customize how Ashes looks?

Yes, Ashes offers several customization options:

  • Light/Dark Mode: Choose between light and dark themes based on your preference
  • Time Units: Select which units of time you want to see (years, months, days, hours, minutes, seconds)
  • Display Options: Adjust the size and prominence of different elements
  • Quotes: Toggle the display of philosophical quotes about time and life

More customization options are planned for future updates, including custom backgrounds and color schemes.

Does Ashes work offline?

Yes! Ashes is designed to work completely offline. Once installed, all calculations happen locally in your browser, and no internet connection is required for the core functionality.

This means your countdown will continue to work even when you're not connected to the internet, making it reliable regardless of your connection status.

Can I see inspirational quotes with Ashes? (Coming Soon!)

Yes, Ashes includes a collection of carefully selected philosophical quotes, primarily from Stoic philosophers, that complement the theme of mortality awareness and intentional living.

These quotes rotate periodically and provide additional context and perspective to the time visualization. You can enable or disable the quotes display in the extension settings.

Privacy & Security

Does Ashes collect my personal data?

No. Ashes is designed with privacy as a core principle. The extension:

  • Stores your birthdate and preferences locally on your device only
  • Does not transmit any personal information to any servers
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not require any account creation or login

Your data remains completely private and under your control at all times. For full details, please see our Privacy Policy.

Is my birthdate information secure?

Yes. Your birthdate is stored using your browser's built-in local storage mechanism and never leaves your device. It's used solely to calculate your remaining time and is not accessible to anyone else, including the extension's creator.

If you ever want to remove this data, you can simply reset the extension's settings or uninstall it entirely.

What permissions does Ashes require?

Ashes requires minimal permissions to function:

  • New Tab Override: To replace your browser's default new tab page
  • Storage: To save your preferences locally on your device

The extension does not request permissions for:

  • Browsing history
  • Website data access
  • Network requests
  • Bookmarks or other browser data

Technical Questions

Which browsers support Ashes?

Ashes is currently available for:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Brave Browser
  • Opera
  • Vivaldi

Most Chromium-based browsers should be compatible with the Chrome version of the extension. If you're using a different browser and would like to see Ashes supported, please let us know through the Feedback form.

Does Ashes slow down my browser?

No. Ashes is designed to be extremely lightweight and efficient. It uses minimal resources and loads instantly when you open a new tab.

The extension:

  • Doesn't load external resources
  • Uses efficient time calculation algorithms
  • Has no background processes when not in use
  • Is optimized for performance

You should notice no difference in your browser's performance with Ashes installed compared to the default new tab page.

Can I use Ashes alongside other new tab extensions?

Unfortunately, no. Browsers only allow one extension to override the new tab page at a time. If you install multiple new tab extensions, only the most recently activated one will be shown.

If you want to switch between different new tab experiences, you'll need to enable/disable the extensions as needed through your browser's extension management page.

Is Ashes open-source?

No, Ashes is proprietary software developed by Aryan Techie/AROICE. Its source code is not publicly available.

However, we welcome feedback and suggestions for improvements through our Feedback form.

Support & Troubleshooting

I just installed Ashes but it's not showing up when I open a new tab.

If Ashes isn't appearing when you open a new tab, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Make sure the extension is enabled in your browser's extension menu
  2. Check if another new tab extension is active and might be overriding Ashes
  3. Restart your browser completely
  4. If using Chrome, go to chrome://extensions, find Ashes, and confirm "Allow in Incognito" is not enabled if you're not in incognito mode
  5. Uninstall and reinstall the extension

If these steps don't resolve the issue, please contact us through the Feedback form with details about your browser and operating system.

How do I update my birth date or preferences?

To update your birth date or other preferences:

  1. Open a new tab to access Ashes
  2. Click the Menu icon in the top right corner of the screen
  3. Access the settings panel where you can update your birth date and other preferences
  4. Save your changes

Your new settings will be applied immediately and stored locally in your browser.

How do I completely reset Ashes?

To completely reset Ashes and all its stored data:

  1. You can do this either by going to the options page of extension, clicking the icon of extenion from extension list and then scroll to botoom then you will see a reset to defaults button
  2. or simply you can just reinstall extension by chrome extensions web store

This will remove all your settings and preferences, allowing you to start fresh the next time you open a new tab.
